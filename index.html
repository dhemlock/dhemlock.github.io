About Us

Hemlock Process Servers was estabilished in North Bay to provide a direct source for serving documents in our community and the surrounding area. Run and staffed by retired members of the OPP, our team of process servers has a wealth of legal knowledge and we understand the importance of due diligence, proper note taking, and providing "court-proof services".
